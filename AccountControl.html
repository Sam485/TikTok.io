Account Control

In this section we will show you how to control your TikTok Account.

Account Control

Login and SignUp

- Get into TikTok and Create your account with the secure password.

- If you don't have any account yet please choose (SIGN UP) you can sign up with many option such as: email,phone number or other platform.

Edit Profile

- Get into your profile then go to edit profile.

- In here you can change your account's name, profile picture and your BIO.

Privacy Settings

Go to (Setting and Privacy) to manage your account privacy.

You can either turn on or turn off Function "Private Account" for your account control.

You can also control on who can comment on your video or who can follow you on TikTok.

Interaction Tools

Shared your video to other social media platform

Find the solution to your problem or making new friends.

use function "Analytics" (For Pro Account) to follow up something that important to you

Screen Time Management

TikTok also provide the function for control the watch tiem on you personal account

Get into "Digital Wellbeing" for control the time that you use TikTok.
